A good tutorial shows the mistake the reader is about to make and gives them a way back. I care about writing only when it changes the work someone has to do.

## The Useful Version

The move that matters for writing is small: connect the idea to a good tutorial shows the mistake the reader is about to make and gives them a way back. Once writing is visible, the reader can tell whether this claim is operational or just commentary.

## The Failure Mode

Writing breaks when the reader can copy the answer without understanding why it worked. Once writing reaches that point, the language may still sound confident, but the system has stopped giving the reader evidence.

## The Test

The version I would publish needs the mistake the tutorial helps them recover from. Without that evidence for writing, I would rather cut the paragraph than decorate the uncertainty.

This works best as a concise note. The writing claim does not need more volume; it needs one clear boundary and one concrete reason to care.
