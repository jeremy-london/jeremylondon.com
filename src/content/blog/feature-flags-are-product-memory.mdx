## What Changes

For feature flags, I would start from the constraint, not the announcement. The constraint is Flags are not just rollout switches. ## Where It Fails

The thing I would watch for with feature flags is quieter than a crash: the feature works in the happy path but leaves the user guessing at the boundary. That is the kind of break that makes a feature flags post feel padded even when the topic is legitimate.

## The Check

My bar for feature flags is the state the interface needs to make visible. If the feature flags post cannot show that, it should stay short and honest.

That is where I would leave it. Keep the feature flags claim narrow, make the tradeoff visible, and let the example do the work.
