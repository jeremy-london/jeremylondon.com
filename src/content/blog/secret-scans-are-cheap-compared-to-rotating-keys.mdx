## The Practical Part

## The Edge Case

The failure mode for security is the happy path works while the unsafe path stays unnamed. For security, that can look fine from a distance, so the page ships, the demo runs, or the team agrees with itself while the next person still cannot tell what actually happened.

## What I Want To See

The check for security is the place where permission, evidence, or ownership is checked. For security, that is enough to separate a useful note from a long one.

I do not want security to sound more certain than the evidence allows. Make the decision visible, then stop.
